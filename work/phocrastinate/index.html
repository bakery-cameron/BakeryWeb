Backstory

We worked with a start up with nothing but an idea - create a platform that delivers a single reel of revolving photos to the world. With this is mind we developed a brand and product that embody the very concept of sharing and lively community.

The Brand

With so much intent packed behind a simple idea and interface, branding posed a unique challenge - to condense Phocrastinate’s values of community, contribution, and recreation into a simple, strong mark.

Web Experience

Keeping in line with Phocrastinate’s mission, the application interface is designed to be simple and minimal, with the photo reel commanding attention above all else. Clear but unobtrusive elements lead users to other features of the website.

Results

Killer brand engagement. We created a global platform with a simple purpose from its core idea to its interface and execution that generated thousands of repeat visitors from all over the world; bringing everyone together one photo at a time.
