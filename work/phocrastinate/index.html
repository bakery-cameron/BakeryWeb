Backstory

The brainchild of Executive Chef Jesse Perez, Arcade Midtown Kitchen, is quickly garnering praise from all around the country and was recently named as one of the top 40 new restaurants of 2013 by Eater.com. At Bakery, we knew it was our task to spread the word of such delicious food. The way we did that was by combining contemporary design with pinches of vintage aesthetics into Arcade’s website, photography, illustration, animation and apparel.
