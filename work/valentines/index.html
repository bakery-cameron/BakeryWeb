Valentine's is kind of a drag, so we decided to spoil it.

Backstory

Highschool sweethearts aside, no one really enjoys Valentine’s Day. For couples it bring an unwelcome pressure to please and for singles it simply served as a reminder of their situation. With this in mind, Bakery set out to create the most pleasurable Valentines experience we could imagine, Wrecking Valentine’s. We’d empower users to take classic Valentine’s staples and burn, crush, smash, and generally demolish all the holiday’s most cringe inducing cliches.

Creating an identity

When it came to giving Wreck Valentine’s an identity we explored many options, playing with the idea of an elegant, feminine look to contrast with the comically brutal nature of the project.

web experience

Using the visual language we established and straightforward intention in mind, we designed a simple, beautiful experience that intuitively allows users wreck Valentine’s.

Our videos were shared all over social media.
