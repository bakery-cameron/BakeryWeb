Waiting a couple seconds can save your life

Challenge

Union Pacific has always been an advocate of safety. However, most of train related accidents happen at a railroad crossing when people pass the gate arms and try to beat the train. Our job was to create a safety campaign that would make the consumer realize that the smart thing to do is to wait.

Strategy

Craft a social media campaign that would embody Union Pacific’s safety message. We tactically tackled two channels within social media that best helped us deliver our campaign to our audience.

YouTube

We realize that YouTube was the perfect channel for us, since whenever an ad comes up before your video, you have to wait a couple seconds before you can skip it. The time the user has to wait in order to skip the ad, would mimic the time he would have to wait at a crossing.

Once the user has waited a couple seconds, we hit them with the message and then encourage them to skip the ad.

3

The ad continues to show the user that after waiting a couple seconds, life continues. Furthermore, that their life is worth the wait.

Facebook

The campaign was extended to Facebook, where the storytelling was made more personable. Our Facebook content video series depicts different scenarios in which a jogger, a young couple and a father and son have each stopped, waiting for a train to pass. When the arms lift, each story proceeds safely toward these individuals' spectacular future.

Production

After a couple days with an awesome crew, long hours, Texas heat, untimely rain and a bunch of re-takes, we ended up with an incredible variety of content that showed Union Pacific’s message.

Bring stories to life

Results

Aside from some beautiful content, we innovated in the way a media can deliver a message and gained free media coverage. But most importantly, helped spread Union’s Pacific safety message Nationwide.
