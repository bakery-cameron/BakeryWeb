As Bakery’s VP of Snuggles, I take my job really seriously, but sometimes my clients and coworkers wonder what exactly I do all day. So I decided to let them keep track of my whereabouts in this “day in the life” video. I hope that you like it and learn a lot about what it means to be a VP of Snuggles.

To sum up my responsibilities, I’m in charge of:

  • Checking up on my coworkers when they are in meetings
  • Making sure everyone takes a break when they are very busy
  • Showing the other dogs how to behave

      • I enjoy working at the Bakery because I have the best coworkers in the world. They give me nice pets and dress me up when they are bored. I like the attention a lot. I also like seeing them smile. It’s not the toughest job in the world, but someone’s gotta do it, and I am glad it is me!

      Alfie

      VP of Snuggles
