Wizard World

Comic Con

This year I attended Austin’s Comic Con Wizard World, full of comic and pop- culture fans, celebrities, and artists. I came across many cosplayers, attended a couple of panels, and some awesome art. Take a look at some of the pictures I took at this 3 day event.

One of the vendors at Wizard World was creating personal action figures. Using a 3D image capturing system, he was able to scan the person and print an action figure of the using a 3D printer.

At Wizard World, I also came across Frankly, which is a new personal chat application. Wizard World attendees used the app to receive all of the exclusive activities and special giveaways, while safely and privately connecting with other fellow fans in attendance. With Frankly, what happened at Wizard World, stayed at Wizard World.

I look forward to see how new technology can be used at the next Wizard World, with 3D printing and interactive chatting who knows what’s next.
