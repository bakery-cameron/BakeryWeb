Apple's new iOS 7 comes with a slew of new features. One that doesn't do much in terms of practicality (or battery life), but is still very cool, is the “parallax effect”. Basically, if you tilt and turn your phone around, your screen shifts the background image around making it feel as though the user interface is floating in a magical land of digital awesome. Such a cool feature caught my attention, and naturally, I had to create my own parallax images to place behind my iPhone's dash, because who the hell does "default" anyway.

I've put together a couple of iOS 7-optimized wallpapers for you to use as you desire. Simply download one of these files and install as your Home background. I hope you like them.

A special thanks goes out to Arcade Midtown Kitchen for letting me use a couple of images for some tasty backgrounds.
