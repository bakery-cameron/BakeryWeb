Bakery is an integrated creative and technology agency. We invent and produce the next big connected experiences for some of the world’s boldest companies.

Our multidisciplinary, intercultural squad is driven to distill your brand’s character and expand it’s cultural reach. We collaborate exclusively with forward-thinking brands interested in growing their relationship and influence with their customers.

Why Bakery?

01

Leaner, Faster, Stronger

Bakery is a lean independent agency, able to move quicker from strategy to production. We iterate effectively by having our senior talent always involved in the work, allowing us to publish and optimize campaigns and products at the speed of today’s market while removing the unnecessary red tape seen in traditional agencies.

02

We’re Way Ahead Of You

We work with organizations that have bold ambitions and are looking for a partner that will explore and execute new ideas. Our focus and strength is creative innovation, which we spur by investing a significant portion of our time and resources to explore valuable opportunities in emerging areas of communications and technology. So, when it’s time to innovate for your brand, we’re months ahead of the competition.

03

No Buffers

We don’t staff an Account Department. You’ll collaborate directly with the folks producing the work. Our goal is to build chemistry with your brand and spark a true partnership between you and our team.
