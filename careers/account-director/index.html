Client Services

Account Director

Job Description

Our Account team plays a pivotal role within our relationships with our clients — on one hand, they are the client’s internal voice at the agency, representing their brand, vision, and best interests, and on the other they’re deeply knowledgeable about Bakery’s services and expertise so they can recommend the best possible solutions and strategic consulting.

We are looking for a seasoned Accounts person to join said team and roll up their sleeves with the rest of us.

Primary
Responsibilites

  • Manage multiple client relationships and engagements.
  • Work with the Production team to establish budget, timetables, and workflow in order to ensure your client’s work is navigating towards a single vision.
  • Give support to agency teams in the form of information, well-written briefs and accurately translating client feedback.
  • Submerge yourself in your client’s business and help marshal them through their data and key trends & patterns to uncover new opportunities that may lead to success.
  • Become the client’s voice and brand ambassador within the organization.
  • Be “always on” and available to manage multiple engagements.

Qualifications

  • 3-5 years of work experience within an agency or client side organization having helped businesses develop winning strategies.
  • Encyclopedic knowledge of advertising models, digital platforms, current technology and best practices in order to solve client challenges.
  • A record of successfully building long-term relationships out of one-time engagements.
  • Experience leading teams through complex business problems.
  • Strong oral and written communication skills.
  • Ability to stay calm and rational, especially when no one else is.
  • General knowledge of tech platforms and their limitations.

Bonus Points

  • A bachelor’s degree. MBA is a plus.
  • Ability to write in English and Spanish is a huge bonus.
  • Fluent in English and Spanish is a plus.

Team diversity is strongly supported!

Bakery is a minority-owned company and an equal opportunity employer.
