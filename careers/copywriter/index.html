Job Description

As a Bakery Content Writer, you would be responsible for the general direction of the brand voice and overall copy associated with our work. In collaboration with Art Directors and under the guidance of Creative Directors and Product Owners, you would help write unique concepts for film, social, web, and other digital possibilities.

We are looking for a highly creative thinker who knows how to leverage strategy and insights, as well as existing technology, to create ideas that trap audiences’ attentions.

Primary
Responsibilites

  • Work with teams to develop a wide variety of interactive, video, and digital projects.
  • Support the efforts of the senior members of our creative staff. Take direction from Creative Directors and Product Owners.
  • Generate storyboards that outline interaction, animation, and/or motion graphics.
  • Develop creative design solutions for limited screen real estate and mobile.
  • Create initial concepts, functional design solutions, site grids, and interface demos.
  • Develop interactive prototypes of new websites, products, and application experiences.
  • Stay current with design and industry trends.

Qualifications

  • Professional writing experience crafting high-quality written content and concepts within a creative or agency environment.
  • Online portfolio of work demonstrating an expertise in content writing with experience working on a variety of projects, namely in film, social, and web content.
  • A restlessly creative mind is a must! At Bakery we’re asked to innovate on a regular basis, not tread the beaten path. This means you must have opinions and ideas, along with a voice to express them properly and within the context of a brief.
  • Grammar and spelling authority.
  • Experience developing successful concepts working in collaborative multi-disciplinary teams.
  • Strong oral and written communication skills.
  • Ability to lead and mentor other writers.
  • General knowledge of tech platforms and their limitations.

Bonus Points

  • A degree in English Language, Journalism or Communications is strongly preferred.
  • Ability to write in English and Spanish is a huge bonus.
  • Fluent in English and Spanish is a plus.
  • Avid comic book reader, movie buff, or die-hard fan of an NBA team.

Team diversity is strongly supported!

Bakery is a minority-owned company and an equal opportunity employer.
