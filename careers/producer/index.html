Producer

Job Description

Our Producers are at the center of our teams and are the gatekeepers of our production process. They manage our projects, of scales large and small, from initial conception through to implementation and launch. Working across several projects of a variety of disciplines at any given time - whether it’s a tech-only project, filming spots for broadcast, or an integrated campaign - they excel at planning and prioritizing all deliverables and team resources. Because Producers must constantly manage client expectations, schedules, budgets, assets and scopes, organization and attention to detail is crucial.

At Bakery, we have semi-autonomous teams, each with individuals representing strategy, creative, and tech. Our Producers are assigned to one of these teams and work as one of its core leaders, ushering these teams to create the beautiful and detailed work Bakery has come to be known for.

As our newest Producer, you’re expected to make informed decisions and act swiftly to find solutions to the problems your team faces in order to ensure the work gets shipped while meeting Bakery’s and your standards.

Primary
Responsibilites

  • Develop and manage project scope, budget and schedules. Your main role is to ensure you deliver a product or campaign on time and within quality standards and budget specifications.
  • Lead your team throughout entire production process - through strategy, creative and development - making sure communication is frequent and collaboration is efficient.
  • Ability to motivate, support and understand your teams. You must be able to easily work with everyone from our visual designers, to our tech developers, to our strategists.
  • Identify resource requirements, required skill sets and the allocation of team members to a project’s core team.
  • Communicate with client side stakeholders on a regular basis and make sure theirs and their project’s needs are being met.
  • Deliver timely project reports and organize necessary meetings.
  • Direct and maintain strict project documentation.
  • Improve and implement best practice techniques.
  • Track and report project efficiency, finances and general performance on a weekly basis.
  • Coordinate efforts with outside vendors.

Qualifications

  • 3+ years of experience in project management, digital / broadcast production, line production, website development or online advertising.
  • Clear understanding of Agile development methods and best practices.
  • Expert knowledge of production cycles for software, print and film.
  • Demonstrated organizational skills and rigorous attention to detail.
  • Unmatched problem-solving skills.
  • Proven ability to collaborate, promote communication, and inspire teamwork between a group of multidisciplinary talent.
  • Excellent knowledge of budgeting, resource scheduling and time management in relation to a project.
  • Experience managing web technology, back-end, application and hosting-related projects as well as mobile, social, SEO/SEM and display campaigns.
  • Working experience with Microsoft Office and Basecamp. Knowledge of Jira, Azana, and Redmine are a plus.
  • A Michael Jordanesque drive to “get things done” no matter what.
  • Impeccable communication and presentation skills with the ability to present to clients and internal stakeholders.

Bonus Points

  • Previous agency, game or software company experience.
  • Fluent in English and Spanish is a big plus.

Team diversity is strongly supported!

Bakery is a minority-owned company and an equal opportunity employer.
