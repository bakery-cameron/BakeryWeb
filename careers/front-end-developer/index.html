Front-End Developer

Job Description

Bakery’s developers are like Santa Claus; they make dreams a reality. Working closely with our creative and design crew, they produce content and products that not only grow business, but also improve people’s lives.

To join said team, we’re looking for a HTML, CSS, and Javascript expert who takes pride in building awesome products through high performing, well-written code. You should love a good challenge and have an eye for good design.

Primary
Responsibilites

  • Oversee the Front-End development of a client solution from start to finish.
  • Provide creative teams with technical guidance with respect to possible solutions to client issues.
  • Interpreting design specifications and producing functional code on test environments as well as client sites.
  • Identify technical risk and communicate with software architecture and production teams.
  • Work closely with other Creatives and other Developers on projects ensuring peer-to-peer sharing of knowledge and practices.
  • Maintain high coding standards and practices and exercising quality control on all aspects of development.
  • Work closely with UX and visual designers to ensure optimal fulfillment of creative objectives and user experience requirements.

Job Requirements

  • 3+ years experience with digital products such as sites and applications.
  • Mastery of HTML and CSS, the latest functionality and applicable polyfills.
  • Expert JavaScript skills and strong knowledge of at least one popular application framework and library such as Angular, Backbone, Ember, etc.
  • Must be able to write modular, reusable, testable, performant code.
  • Experience with CSS preprocessors such as SASS or LESS.
  • Strong understanding of the DOM and how it functions (e.g. Reflow, Repaint, etc).
  • Experience with version control such as Git.
  • Experience with common bootstrap or build tools (eg. Yeoman, Middleman, Grunt).
  • Experience solving compatibility issues between less advanced browsers.
  • Comfortable operating from a command line interpreter (e.g. Terminal).
  • Experience developing responsive or mobile websites.
  • Experience delivering websites that adhere to SEO/ Accessibility best practices.

Bonus Points

  • Back-end experience in NodeJS, AngularJS, Java, .Net, or Ruby on Rails.
  • Working knowledge of Play Framework and Scala a big plus.
  • Experience with testing environments such as Jasmine and Karma.
  • Contributor to open source projects.
  • Fluent in English and Spanish is a plus.

Team diversity is strongly supported!

Bakery is a minority-owned company and an equal opportunity employer.
