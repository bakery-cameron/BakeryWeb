Art Director

Job Description

Bakery Art Directors are responsible for driving the visual aspects of our projects. They are designers, artists and illustrators, but most of all, they are conceptors with an ability to capture people’s imaginations.

As a Bakery AD, whether you’re creating the design direction for an online commercial or polishing the next amazing web experience, you will apply your unique creative thinking and craft to delivering projects from lowly brief to inspiring consumer product or campaign.

Primary
Responsibilites

  • Work with teams to develop a wide variety of interactive, video, and digital projects.
  • Support the efforts of the senior members of our creative staff. Take direction from Creative Directors and Product Owners.
  • Generate storyboards that outline interaction, animation, and/or motion graphics.
  • Develop creative design solutions for limited screen real estate and mobile.
  • Create initial concepts, functional design solutions, site grids, and interface demos.
  • Develop interactive prototypes of new websites, products, and application experiences.
  • Stay current with design and industry trends.

Qualifications

  • Professional art direction and design experience crafting high-quality solutions within a creative or agency environment.
  • Online portfolio of work displaying versatile, tasteful design consistent with or exceeding quality in Bakery’s own portfolio.
  • A restlessly creative mind is a must! At Bakery we’re asked to innovate on a regular basis, not tread the beaten path. This means you must have opinions and ideas, along with a voice to express them properly and within the context of a brief.
  • Must demonstrate intimate knowledge of information design, including semantic information for the web, and user interface issues such as browser / platform constraints and capabilities.
  • Experience developing successful concepts working in collaborative multi-disciplinary teams.
  • Ability to lead and mentor other designers.
  • Mastery of Illustrator, Photoshop, and Indesign. Working knowledge of Macaw and/ or Sketch a big bonus.

Bonus Points

  • A degree in Interaction Design or other Visual Study is strongly preferred.
  • Strong knowledge of HTML, CSS, and other popular web languages.
  • Fluent in English and Spanish is a plus.
  • Avid comic book reader, movie buff, or die-hard fan of an NBA team.

Team diversity is strongly supported!

Bakery is a minority-owned company and an equal opportunity employer.
