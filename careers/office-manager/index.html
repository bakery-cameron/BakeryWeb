Office Manager

Job Description

At Bakery, the Office Manager is also known as the Chief Happiness Officer; they keep the workplace stocked with goodies and supplies, makes sure the team gets fed, handles the wellness and company events schedule, and oversees the general upkeep of our headquarters.

On top of that, the OM serves as one of the physical faces of the company. They open up in the morning, maintain a presence in the office, receives guests and deliveries, and answers our main line on a day to day basis.

The OM is also in charge of on-boarding new employees by putting together welcome packages and giving rookies the necessary tour of our facility and the perks that come with it.

Primary
Responsibilites

  • Maintaining office supplies, checking mail, and receiving packages.
  • Managing our food program and catering schedule.
  • Handling office requests and necessary vendors (e.g. handymen).
  • Coordinating office visitors with stakeholders’ schedules.
  • Preparing welcome packages for new employees.
  • Scheduling wellness program with practitioners; yoga and massage.
  • Maintenance of all office equipment (e.g. printers, conference rooms, computers, phones).
  • Supervise cleaning crew.
  • Plan small events such as happy hours and outings.
  • Assist executive staff with travel coordination.

Requirments

  • Superior organizational and planning skills.
  • Experience in an office assistant/admin role.
  • Great phone and conversation skills.
  • Calm and tactful under pressure.
  • Professionalism, poise, diplomacy, and self-confidence to take on difficult issues.
  • Discretion about private and confidential matters.
  • Problem solver who is eager to help out and doesn’t mind random requests.
  • A confident and positive people person.
  • Passion for the industry, competitive spirit, motivated to learn and grow.

Bonus Points

  • A bachelor’s degree.
  • Agency experience.
  • Event/ party planning experience a plus.
  • Ability to write in English and Spanish is a huge bonus.
  • Fluent in English and Spanish is a plus.

Team diversity is strongly supported!

Bakery is a minority-owned company and an equal opportunity employer.
