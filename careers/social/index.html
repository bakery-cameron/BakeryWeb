Social Marketing Strategist

Job Description

Bakery is currently seeking a Social Media Strategist. You will play a key role in helping drive client strategy and project implementation for both current and new clients. You will lead clientsâ€™ social media initiatives, including: planning, implementation and measurement of social campaigns, brand monitoring and reputation management, content development and paid/owned/earned distribution. The position requires an in-depth knowledge of strategic planning and engagement capabilities across established and emerging social networks, and the role that those channels play within a broader marketing ecosystem and business transformation.

Primary
Responsibilites

  • Design, develop, and present comprehensive strategic online and social media plan initiatives
  • Provide leadership, direction, and relevant insights that will guide the social media efforts and integrated marketing communications planning
  • Deliver compelling competitive analysis, audience analysis, industry trends, and client-facing and internal direction to identify and address key social media challenges and solutions
  • Synthesize analysis to make powerful recommendations that drive clients to action and excite clients
  • Apply knowledge to develop social media marketing plans that add value to the brand and identify future opportunities within the social media space
  • Provide thought leadership and drive innovation across the various engagements
  • Implement strategy across social channels, monitor and optimize campaigns to drive engagement across projects

Qualifications

  • Qualified candidates will bring at least 3 years social/ digital experience within an agency or similar setting
  • Must be able to work in a multi-discipline collaborative environment and have a deep knowledge of social media technologies, platforms, and services.
  • Team management and client facing experience a must.
  • Extremely strong leader with the ability to manage multiple initiatives
  • Demonstrate strong verbal, interpersonal, and written communication skills
  • Display a professional, detail-oriented and proactive approach

Bonus Points

  • Experience with two or more industry sectors is strongly preferred (e.g. CPG, Hospitality, Automotive, Beer & Alcoholic Beverages).
  • Multi-channel media experience (e.g. digital advertising, websites and digital outdoor).
  • Being bilingual (English and Spanish) is a huge plus.
  • UX design experience is a bonus.

Team diversity is strongly supported!
