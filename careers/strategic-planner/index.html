Strategic Planner

Job Description

We’re looking for an expert strategist to join our team, contributing elite skill in brand planning and storytelling. We need this person to be well versed in current and upcoming digital platforms, as well as a friendly collaborator with our Creative and Production teams.

The right candidate will demonstrate a strong understanding of trends, data and insight, as well as how to use these to produce incremental results for our clients. Our Strategic Planner would have ownership of all internal research and the methodologies that can lead Bakery to generate original insights for existing and prospective clients.

We’re looking for a true team member that wants to provide growth for our client and our agency just as strongly as they do for themselves; someone who won’t just address the current issues, but will also proactively seek out opportunities to improve the business.

Primary
Responsibilites

  • Conduct, moderate, and oversee user research across various projects.
  • Collaborate with Creative and Production teams to set goals and ensure quality research and the highest possible quality level in our work.
  • Work with clients to identify specific business opportunities, leverage findings from their existing research and then apply the knowledge to develop powerful creative and communications strategies.
  • Develop and own our creative briefs and partner with Creative, Production, Media and other teams to ensure we stay true to the strategic vision and voice though campaign development and rollout.
  • Eloquently present any and all communications strategies internally as well as to existing and prospective clients.
  • Evangelize the value of user research internally and to potential clients.

Qualifications

  • Extensive experience (4+ years) in strategic planning within a digital or integrated agency environment.
  • Experience with various research methodologies and tools and a good sense of when to use which.
  • Successful track record of delivering strategy projects, preferably with influential brands.
  • True partner and counselor to creative teams to help them develop excellent work.
  • Proven success in developing big ideas that forge strong connections between brand and consumer.
  • Deep understanding of modern communication channels and trends, and a passion for digital solutions involving customers in interactions with a brand.
  • Great group moderation skills.
  • Ability to present information clearly and confidently, especially in short periods of time.
  • Professionalism, poise, diplomacy, and self-confidence to take on difficult issues.

Bonus Points

  • Experience with two or more industry sectors is strongly preferred (e.g. CPG, Hospitality, Automotive, Beer & Alcoholic Beverages).
  • Multi-channel media experience (e.g. digital advertising, websites and digital outdoor).
  • Being bilingual (English and Spanish) is a huge plus.
  • UX design experience is a bonus.

Team diversity is strongly supported!

Bakery is a minority-owned company and an equal opportunity employer.
